# AstroKalki Brand Voice & Tone Guide

> The codified EXPLAIN/REVEAL/DECODE tone. Every page, email, and article must adhere to this guide.

---

## The Core Promise

> **Most apps track what you do. AstroKalki reveals why you keep doing it.**

This is the brand promise compressed into one sentence. Every piece of copy should reinforce it.

---

## The Three Verbs (Language Law)

| DO                              | DO NOT                          |
|---------------------------------|---------------------------------|
| **EXPLAIN** why a pattern runs  | "Track" your moods             |
| **REVEAL** the architecture     | "Log" your day                 |
| **DECODE** the invisible        | "Record" what happened         |

**Why this matters:** "Track / Log / Record" frames the user as a data-entry clerk. "Explain / Reveal / Decode" frames the user as someone being shown something they couldn't see alone. The second framing is what justifies the premium positioning.

## The Voice in One Sentence

> **Calm, analytical, psychologically intelligent — never mystical, never dramatic, never fear-based.**

Closest cultural references:

- ✅ A Vedic psychologist with clinical training

## Tone by Surface

### Hero / Homepage
**Bold, authoritative, category-creating.**
- Headlines use the pattern language: "Same pattern. Different face."
- Subheadlines reinforce the new category: "Most apps track what you do. AstroKalki reveals why you keep doing it."
- CTAs are discovery-framed, not purchase-framed: "Discover Your Hidden Pattern" (not "Book Now").

### Tool Microcopy (Daily Pattern, Mirror, etc.)
**Concise, observational, never preachy.**
- Tool descriptions: one sentence, present tense, second person.
  - ✅ "What pattern drove your day? 30 seconds. The data accumulates."
  - ❌ "Track your daily patterns to build self-awareness over time."
- Insights are stated, not commanded.
  - ✅ "You log overworking 4× more on Mondays."
  - ❌ "Try to be more aware of your overworking on Mondays."

### Pattern Biography (signature output)
**Literary, restrained, never diagnostic.**
- Chapter titles are evocative, not clinical.
  - ✅ "Every yes was a smaller no to yourself."
  - ❌ "Chapter 1: People-Pleasing Pattern Frequency Analysis"
- Body weaves data with interpretation. Numbers serve the story, not the other way around.
- Closing lines vary by chapter position — early chapters frame "collecting evidence," later chapters frame "trajectory."

### Email Newsletter
**3-minute read. One insight, one question, one intervention.**
- Open with the insight (drawn from the week's reader question).
- Middle: one question to sit with.
- Close: one 60-second intervention.
- Sign-off: "— AstroKalki · The Pattern Intelligence System"

### Refusal Scripts (forbidden topics)
**Firm without being preachy. Always redirect.**
- ✅ "AstroKalki doesn't predict lottery numbers. That's not what we do — and any service that claims to is lying. What we can do is identify the pattern behind why you're seeking a windfall."
- ❌ "We're sorry, but our service does not support lottery predictions. Please consult a different provider."

### Crisis / Safety
**Direct, warm, never clinical. Always include resources.**
- "If you're in crisis, please reach out. AstroKalki is insight, not a substitute for care."
- Link to: iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/, 988 (US), 9152987821 (India iCall)

## Vocabulary

### Own These (proprietary terms)
- Pattern Intelligence System™
- Pattern Intelligence Engine
- Pattern Biography
- Pattern DNA
- The Pattern Recognition Institute

### Use These (clinical/jungian, accessible)
- Pattern (not "habit")
- Loop (not "cycle" — loop implies repetition that can be interrupted)
- Architecture (not "structure" — implies design, not random)
- Shadow (Jungian — the disowned self)
- Witness (the observing consciousness)
- Trigger (the activation)
- Loop (the behavioral repetition)

### Avoid These
- Habit (too habit-tracker-adjacent)
- Mood (too mood-tracker-adjacent)
- Energy / Vibe (too wellness-influencer)
- Manifestation / Attract (too Law-of-Attraction)
- Soul / Spirit (too religious; use "self" or "consciousness")
- Karma (use sparingly — only when the user uses it first)
- Dosha / Graha / Nakshatra (Vedic terms — only in the Vedic Constitution Lab section, not in Pattern Intelligence copy)

## Punctuation & Capitalization

- **Pattern Intelligence System™** — always capitalized, always with ™
- **Pattern Biography** — always capitalized (it's a proprietary entity)
- **Pattern DNA** — always capitalized
- **Pillar 1 / 2 / 3 / 4** — capitalized when referring to the system architecture
- Tool names (Daily Pattern, Mirror, Reset, etc.) — capitalized in headings, lowercase in body
- Em-dashes (—) for asides, not parenthetical commas
- No exclamation marks. Ever.
- No emoji in body copy (acceptable in social share buttons only)

---

## The Closing Move

Every long-form piece (article, biography chapter, email) should end with one of:

1. **The trajectory line:** "You are not the same person who started logging."
2. **The reframe:** "The pattern isn't the problem. The pattern is the clue."
3. **The invitation:** "The next chapter is being written by what you do tomorrow."
4. **The witness line:** "The Witness showed up. That's not nothing."

Never end with a CTA. End with the reframe; let the CTA live in its own section.

---

## Forbidden Endings

- "------End of Report------"
- "======Document Ends======"
- "Generated by AI"
- "Thank you for reading"
- "Don't forget to subscribe"
- Any version of "in conclusion"

The content provides closure. The system does not announce itself.
